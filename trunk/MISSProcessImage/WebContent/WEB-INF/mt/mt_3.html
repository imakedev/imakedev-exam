Customer Mind Report Result:
Customer Mind ability is the result of emotion management. Emotions are very complex things For example, when you experience an emotion such as anger, several different responses occur almost simultaneously. You also may experience a variety of physical urges including the desire to hit something, purchases something, or get away form the situation. There may also be a physiological component such as crying or shaking. However if you have a good emotion management, you will control such habits and can work or handle with situation with efficiency. The Customer Mind testing is the test that find the person who are best in controlling or managing their emotion with efficiency.
The Customer Mind testing is designed to help organization to explore people or person about their emotion management for put them in the right position or to handle the work that need for customer mind ability as the key of work performance.
